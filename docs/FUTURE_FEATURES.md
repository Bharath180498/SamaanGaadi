# Future Features (Premium Versions)

This document lists upgrade ideas for paid plans across driver, customer, and your admin panel.

## 1) Driver Pro / Driver Max

- Heat maps for high-demand zones by hour/day (live + forecast).
- Surge opportunity alerts ("move 2 km here, likely +X trips in next 30 mins").
- Smarter job filters (minimum fare, preferred distance, preferred neighborhoods).
- Multi-stop optimization suggestions to reduce empty return trips.
- Earnings intelligence (best hours, best zones, acceptance impact simulator).
- Priority support queue for paid drivers.

## 2) Customer Premium

- Faster matching priority during peak demand.
- Smart rebooking suggestions after cancellation.
- Saved frequent routes + one-tap repeat booking.
- Price-drop alerts for favorite routes/time windows.
- Premium support response lane.
- Advanced trip history insights (monthly spend, top routes, repeat drivers).

## 3) Admin Premium Ops (Single-Owner Control Panel)

- Live demand heatmap + driver density overlay (city/zone level).
- Supply-demand imbalance alerts and auto recommendation engine.
- Predictive shortage alerts for upcoming peak hours.
- Smart pricing recommendations by micro-zone and time slot.
- Escalation automations for long-wait support tickets.
- Fraud/risk dashboard (suspicious trips, payment disputes, abuse patterns).

## 4) QargoAI Premium Add-ons

- AI dispatch copilot with suggested actions and one-click execution.
- AI support summarizer + suggested replies with confidence labels.
- AI churn predictor (drivers/customers likely to drop off).
- AI growth copilot (which zones to push promos/incentives in).
- Natural-language analytics ("Show cancellations in East Bangalore this week").

## 5) Payments & Financial Features

- Instant payout option (paid add-on per transfer or monthly plan).
- Driver cashflow view: expected payouts, pending payouts, settlement history.
- Auto-reconciliation for gateway payments and dispute matching.
- Wallet credits/rewards for high-quality completion behavior.

## 6) Safety & Trust Features

- Trip risk scoring and anomaly detection for high-risk deliveries.
- Mandatory proof workflows by risk tier (photo/signature/OTP).
- Driver trust score improvements based on quality/compliance signals.
- Business customer trust profiles and repeat behavior scoring.

## 7) Recommended Packaging (Simple)

- Free: basic booking + trip management.
- Pro: heatmaps, analytics, advanced filters, faster support.
- Max: AI tools, automations, advanced insights, instant payouts add-ons.

## 8) Suggested Build Order

- Phase 1: Driver heatmaps + demand alerts + premium support queue.
- Phase 2: Admin demand/supply intelligence + pricing recommendations.
- Phase 3: Customer premium intelligence + retention features.
- Phase 4: Full QargoAI premium automation suite.
